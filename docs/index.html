If I consider the thinking
I do privately to be done
at home, then I imagine this
place to be the little cedar-
-shingled studio I’ve built to
learn and make in the backyard.

It’s warm cozy.
When you come inside,
you’ll see, tools strewn about.

Some of the tools are prototypes
I’ve made...artifacts of moments
along a journey through something.

There are also tools other people
have made that I’ve used
and have inspired me.

Please pick things up. If anything
here prompts thoughts or questions in you,
I'd value you being in touch about them.
