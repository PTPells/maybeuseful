Maybe useful

If I consider the thinking I do privately to be done
at home, then I imagine this place to be the little
cedar-shingled studio I’ve built to learn and make
in the backyard.

It’s warm and cozy. When you come inside you'll
see sketches strewn about. Some sketches are in
notebooks that I haven't revisited in a while, others
are taped to the wall as reminders, and a few are still
warm from the touch of my left-hand.

Most of the sketches you see are prototypes I've made.
...artifacts of moments where I've used what I've had
on hand to think through something.

There are also drawings other people have made in
here. These are tools that I’ve used and have
been inspired by.

Please pick up whatever catches your eye. And if something
prompts a thought or question, I'd value you being in
touch about it.
